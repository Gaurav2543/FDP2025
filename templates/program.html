Program Schedule

Faculty Development Program 2025

Computational Genomics: Path to Precision Medicine

March 24-28, 2025

FDP 2025 Program Overview

Our four-day program is designed to provide comprehensive training in various aspects of genomics. Each day focuses on specific themes and includes lectures, hands-on sessions, and interactive discussions.

Day 1: Foundations of Modern Genomics

  • 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM: Opening Ceremony and Introduction to FDP 2025
  • 10:45 AM - 12:15 PM: Lecture: Evolution of Genomic Technologies
  • 1:30 PM - 3:00 PM: Hands-on Session: Introduction to NGS Platforms
  • 3:15 PM - 4:45 PM: Panel Discussion: Challenges and Opportunities in Genomics Research

Day 2: Bioinformatics and Data Analysis

  • 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM: Lecture: Principles of Bioinformatics in Genomics
  • 10:45 AM - 12:15 PM: Hands-on Session: Genomic Data Analysis Tools
  • 1:30 PM - 3:00 PM: Workshop: Navigating Genomic Databases
  • 3:15 PM - 4:45 PM: Case Studies: Applying Bioinformatics in Research

Day 3: Advanced Genomic Applications

  • 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM: Lecture: Functional Genomics and Its Applications
  • 10:45 AM - 12:15 PM: Workshop: CRISPR Gene Editing Techniques
  • 1:30 PM - 3:00 PM: Lecture: Epigenomics and Its Implications
  • 3:15 PM - 4:45 PM: Panel Discussion: Ethical Considerations in Genomic Research

Day 4: Future of Genomics

  • 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM: Lecture: Genomic Medicine and Personalized Healthcare
  • 10:45 AM - 12:15 PM: Workshop: Integrating Genomics into Curriculum
  • 1:30 PM - 3:00 PM: Panel Discussion: Future Directions in Genomics Research and Education
  • 3:15 PM - 4:45 PM: Closing Ceremony and Certificate Distribution

Note: This schedule is tentative and subject to minor changes. Detailed session descriptions and speaker information will be provided to registered participants.
