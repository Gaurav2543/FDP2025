GTP 2025

IIIT Hyderabad, India

March 12-15, 2025

IIIT Hyderabad

The Genomics Training Program (GTP) 2025 will be held at the prestigious Indian Institute of Information Technology Hyderabad (IIIT-H), known for its cutting-edge research and state-of-the-art facilities.

Address

Getting There

From Rajiv Gandhi International Airport: The campus is approximately 35 km from the airport. Taxis and pre-paid cab services are available at the airport.

From Secunderabad Railway Station: The campus is about 20 km from the station. Local buses and cabs are available for transportation.

Accommodation

Limited on-campus accommodation is available for participants on a first-come, first-served basis. Additionally, we have partnered with nearby hotels to offer special rates for GTP 2025 attendees. More information on accommodation options will be provided upon registration.

Local Attractions

Hyderabad, known as the "City of Pearls," offers a rich blend of history, culture, and technology. Some popular attractions include:

  • Charminar
  • Golconda Fort
  • Hussain Sagar Lake
  • Ramoji Film City
  • Salar Jung Museum

We encourage participants to explore the city's vibrant culture and cuisine during their stay.
