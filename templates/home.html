About GTP

The Genomics Training Program (GTP) 2025 is an intensive, four-day workshop designed to equip faculty members and research scholars with cutting-edge knowledge and skills in genomics. Led by subject field experts, this program offers a unique opportunity to delve into the latest advancements, techniques, and applications in genomic research.

Topics of Interest

Biological big data analytics
Genome analysis
Computational proteomics
Health informatics
Molecular evolution
Structural bioinformatics
Transcriptomics
Databases and ontologies

And many more cutting-edge topics in computational biology and medical sciences.

Why Attend GTP 2025?

  • Learn from world-renowned experts in genomics
  • Gain hands-on experience with state-of-the-art genomic technologies
  • Network with peers and leaders in the field
  • Explore the latest trends and future directions in genomic research
  • Enhance your teaching and research capabilities in genomics

Who Should Attend

  • Faculty members in life sciences, biotechnology, and related fields
  • Postdoctoral researchers and senior PhD scholars
  • Professionals in genomics and bioinformatics
